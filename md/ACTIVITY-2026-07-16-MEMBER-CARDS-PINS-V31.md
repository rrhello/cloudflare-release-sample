# v31 — Member Cards, Persistent Channels, and Pin Heights

- Member cards use branded gray backgrounds with dark-brown text and spacing
  between cards.
- Profile photos are circular, proportionally cropped, and padded inside a sage
  border.
- Active member filters use dark sage with white text; inactive filters use
  branded gray and dark-brown text.
- Channel rows retain their custom gray/dark-brown treatment across rerenders,
  with sage hover and dark-sage active states.
- Featured carousel slides, links, and cards share an exact 350px height.
- Long featured pin text uses four-line ellipsis and remains contained.
